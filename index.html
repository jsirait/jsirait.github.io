Audience-based news source characterization using probabilistic matrix factorization GitHub repo

I built an interaction data from the share counts of news articles in various subreddits (communities within Reddit), then used PMF algorithms with different priors and approximation techniques to extract latent feature vectors of news sources. I then explored the utility of these latent features in characterizing news sources and identifying similar news sources. Inspired by my undergraduate thesis.
